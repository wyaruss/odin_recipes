Chicken Katsu

A Japanese taked on fried chicken.

An incredible, crispy chicken katsu recipe (I take no credit for this recipe--read the original by sakuraiiko here ).

Ingredients

  • Four skinless, bonless chicken breast halves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Canola oil

Steps (as written by sakuraiiko)

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
